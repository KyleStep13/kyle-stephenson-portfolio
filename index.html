About Me

Who I Am

I'm Kyle Stephenson — a Sports Business student at the University of Oregon's Lundquist College of Business and an aspiring sports agent with hands-on experience across athlete representation, NIL marketing, and professional sports operations.

As Head of Brand Relations and Account Manager at Oregon Accelerator, a student-run sports marketing agency, I help lead campaign execution and connect national brands with collegiate athletes for NIL partnerships. As a College Football Agent Intern with Slice Sports Management, I'm getting hands-on exposure to athlete representation — supporting recruiting, the transfer portal, brand development, and partnership opportunities for collegiate football players.

My experience spans the full sports ecosystem: from the NFL sidelines as an Equipment Intern with the Los Angeles Chargers and a talent scout with the New York Jets' Phil Savage Scout School, to the governance side as Secretary of the Board of Directors for The Duck Store. I'm driven by one goal — empowering athletes to maximize their name, image, and likeness, and building the relationships that shape careers on and off the field.
